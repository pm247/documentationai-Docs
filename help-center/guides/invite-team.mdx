title: Inviting Team Members
description: How to add users to your MyCMO project.
---

## Overview

Collaborate by adding teammates to your project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Select your project from the MyCMO dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Team tab">
    Click the "Team" section in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite members">
    Enter email addresses and click "Send Invite". Members will get an email to join.
  </Step>
</Steps>
